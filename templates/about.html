About Us

About Us

At 3 Realms Entertainment, we’re dedicated to bringing the vibrant world of Indian cinema to European audiences. Based in Germany, our mission is to enrich cultural exchange through carefully curated films, ranging from Bollywood blockbusters to independent gems.

With a commitment to excellence and personalized service, we’re your trusted gateway to Indian cinema, ensuring an unforgettable viewing experience.

Fueled by creativity, integrity, and a passion for cinematic excellence, we aim to positively impact the world through the transformative power of storytelling.

Join us on a journey to discover, celebrate, and share the magic of Indian cinema with audiences far and wide.

Vision & Mission

Vision

To lead the European film distribution sector, introducing audiences to the richness of Indian cinema and using the power of movies to uplift, inform, and amuse.

Mission

We’re dedicated to sharing outstanding Indian films with European audiences, guided by our commitment to creativity, integrity, and a profound love for cinema, aiming to enrich lives and inspire positive change through storytelling.
